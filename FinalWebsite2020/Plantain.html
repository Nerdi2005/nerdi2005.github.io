How to Make Fried Plantain

This is a recipe for how to make plantain the Ghanian way. It is eaten in Ghana by many people and is very loved by many. This meal goes very good with chicken made in the Ghanian style.

Ingredients

  • Canola Oil
  • Deep Fryer
  • Plantain
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Cut up Plantains
  2. Put in Oil for 5 minutes
  3. Take out and let it sit for 5 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
