Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Here is the recipe for making a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. It is a good snack that can be made in very little time and can be taken and eaten anywhere.

Ingredients

  • One jar of peanut butter
  • One jar of jam (your choice)
  • Two slices of bread
  • One butter knife (for spreading)

Instructions

  1. Toast both slices of bread (optional)
  2. Open the jar of peanut butter and spread it on one slice of bread
  3. Open the jar of jam and spread it on the other slice of bread
  4. Take the two slices of bread and put them together so that the peanut butter and jelly are sandwiched
  5. Eat
