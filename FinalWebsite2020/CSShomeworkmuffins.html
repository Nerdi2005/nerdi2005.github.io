Muffins Recipe

This recipe makes 12 classic plain muffins, and can be prepared in 25 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 120 ml sunflower oil
  • 375 mg self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200 g caster sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celcius, and oil 12 muffin cases.
  2. Beat the egg with the oil and milk until well mixed. Mix the flour, baking powder, and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Divide the mixture between the twelve cases and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Transfer the muffins to a wire rack and leave to cool completely when done.
