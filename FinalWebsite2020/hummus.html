The Nagpal Hummus Recipe!

This recipe is the first and only thing I have ever made, and I made it for the first time yesterday. So, I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 30 ounces of chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of tahini
  • 2 small garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons of water
  • 1 spatula
  • 1 food processor
  • Pita bread

Directions

  1. Combine the tahini and the lemon juice inside the food processor.
  2. Process for 1 minute.
  3. Use the spatula to scrape the sides and the bottom of the bowl. This helps to make the hummus smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the olive oil, minced garlic, cumin and salt to the mix.
  5. Process for 30 seconds.
  6. Scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl for 30 seconds.
  7. Process for another 30 seconds.
  8. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  9. Add half of the chickpeas to the mix and process for a minute.
  10. Scrape sides and bottom of the bowl, then add remaining chickpeas
    And process for 1 or 2 minutes, or until thick and smooth.
  11. As it is processing, add 1 tablespoon of water at a time until
    You find that it is smooth and that there are no more tiny bits of chickpea.
  12. Enjoy with some pita bread!
