STRAWBERRY ORANGE POPSICLES RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 3½ Cups Unsweetened Almond Breeze Original
  • 1 Cup orange juice
  • 2 oranges peeled, sectioned
  • ½ Cup frozen strawberries
  • ½ Cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Strawberry Layer: combine 1 Cup Almond Breeze, ½ Cup orange juice, ½ Cup frozen strawberries, ½ Cup fresh strawberries and 1 orange (peeled and sectioned). Blend until smooth. Pour into glass jar and set aside. Keep refrigerated.
  2. Orange Layer: combine 1 Cup Almond Breeze, ½ Cup orange juice, and 1 orange (peeled and sectioned). Blend until smooth. Pour into glass jar and set aside. Keep refrigerated.
  3. (Optional) Vanilla Protein Layer: combine 1½ Cups Almond Breeze and 2 scoops vanilla protein powder. Blend until smooth. Pour into glass jar and set aside. Keep refrigerated.
  4. Pour one flavor into mold. Freeze 30-40 minutes. Continue with each flavor until mold is full. Freeze for 4-6 hours total.

Notes

  • How to make regular popsicles (no layers): Combine all ingredients into a blender, blending until smooth. Pour into popsicle mold. Freeze 4-6 hours.
  • Option of adding fresh slices of strawberries to mold.
