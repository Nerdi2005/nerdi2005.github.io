Cowboy Cookies Recipe

Chocolate chip cookies just got better! Cowboy Cookie are like chocolate chip
but with the added punch of variety!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups of oats
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips (optional)

    • For this recipe feel free to add any other ingredients like a cup of coconut shavings, a handfull of and chocolate candy of your choosing

    Cowboy cookies are great for creating your own version of cookies

Directions

  1. Set oven temperature to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease the baking sheets
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt, set aside to use later
  3. Cream together the butter and both sugars. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla
  4. Stir in the dry ingredients one quarter of the mixture at a time
  5. Stir in the oats, chocolate chips, and any other ingredient you choose
  6. Make small balls and place them on the baking sheet. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes then let cool for 5 minutes
