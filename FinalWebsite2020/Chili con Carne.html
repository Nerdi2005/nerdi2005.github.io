My stepmom's delicious Chili con Carne

Ingredients for one big pot of Chili

  • 1kg minced meat
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 bags chopped tomatoes
  • 5 onions
  • 3-4 fresh red pepperonis
  • 2 tablespoons clarified butter
  • 100g tomato paste
  • 1 tin of kidney beans
  • 1 tin of corn
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoonvegetable stock
  • Salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, basil, cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Dice the bell peppers, onions and pepperonis
  2. Heat clarified butter
  3. Steam the onions until transparent
  4. Add the tomato paste and roast briefly
  5. Brown the minced meat well, then season it with salt and basil
  6. Add the bell peppers, chopped tomatoes, pepperonis, kidney beans and corn, stir in the vegetable stock and let it simmer together
  7. Season to taste with salt, cayenne pepper, paprika, sour cream and cinnamon
  8. Serve with baguette and butter, if you like, and enjoy!
