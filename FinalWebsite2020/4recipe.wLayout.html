Ice Cream Float

by: Mr. Drenth

This is a classic desert from my childhood. Sometimes called a " spider" It mixes well with movies and friends. Make sure you have enough for extras.

Ingredients

  • 2L bottle of pop (any variety)
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 straw
  • 2 spoon
  • 1 large glass

Directions

  1. Open pop bottle
  2. Carefully, pour pop into the glass. Fill it only half-way.
  3. Add ice cream
  4. Eat with spoon or straw
