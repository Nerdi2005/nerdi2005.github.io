Student Style Beef Ramen: A homemade recipe, tried and true

Ingredients

  • One tray of thinly sliced beef
  • One packet of Instant Ramen
  • Some cooking oil
  • 2L of water
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
    •

    Instructions

    1. Boil a pot of water over the stove
    2. Once water is boiling, add only the NOODLES from the Instant Ramen pack in
    3. Gently stir until noodles are firm but not hard
    4. Put ramen into a bowl
    5. On a seperate pan, spread an very thin and even layer of cooking oil onto the surface
    6. Add in the beef with seasoning to taste
    7. Stir until beef is medium done
    8. Add beef onto the ramen. Enjoy!
